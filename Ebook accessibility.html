E-Textbooks Accessibility Evaluations

In this project, I worked as an e-textbook accessibility evaluator for California Open Online Library for Education (COOL4Ed). The critera for e-textbook accessibility was developed according to the web content accessibility guidelines (WCAG 2.0). We developed 15 checkpoints and rated each checkpoint on a scale of 1 - 10. The tools used for these evaluations include Adobe Acrobat Pro DC, Adobe Digital Editions, Windows Narrator, NVDA, the Colour Contrast Analyser, and Google Chrome extensions Care Your Eyes and Select and Speak.

During my two years working in the lab, I evaluated over 50 books in PDF, HTML, and EPUB, and trained 15 students on the accessibility evaluations.
