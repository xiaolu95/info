Co-authorship Network Analysis in Vigilance Research

In this project, we worked as a team to look at the collaborative networks of human factors research, specifically on the topic of vigilance. We wanted to know if the author's centrality is related to one's gender, impact factor, and geographic location. After finding over 100 articles, we recorded the articles and all authors of the articles into an adjacency list and calculated degree centrality, closeness centrality, and betweenness centrality of the network.

Team: K.M. McCoy, H. MacNew, X. Bai, & C. Pham
