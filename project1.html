Take-Overs of Automated Driving System in Virtual Reality Environment

In this study, I worked with other researchers to develop this simulator in a virtual reality environment that mimics level 3 automation. I also helped with analyzing data associated with driving performance during obstacle avoidance. This study compared the performance of take-overs of level 3 automation and level 0 automation (fully manual driving).

A level 3 automation system is able to perform simple driving tasks, but the human driver is expected to respond appropriately to a request to intervene.
